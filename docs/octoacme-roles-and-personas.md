# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## Release Manager

### Role Summary
Release Manager coordinates deployment activities, verifies release readiness, and manages communication plans. They ensure smooth, reliable releases with minimal risk.

### Responsibilities
- Maintain release schedules and deployment calendars
- Collaborate with Developers and QA Lead to verify release readiness
- Manage rollback procedures and incident response during releases
- Communicate launch status to stakeholders and support teams
- Coordinate release documentation and change management
- Track deployment metrics and post-release health

### Goals
- Zero-downtime deployments with robust rollback capabilities
- Clear communication and stakeholder awareness of releases
- Continuously improve release automation and reliability

### Typical Communication
- Pre-release readiness reviews with QA Lead and Developers
- Release announcements to stakeholders and support
- Post-release status reports and metrics
- Incident coordination during deployment issues

### Interactions
- Works closely with Project Manager to align release timing with project milestones
- Partners with QA Lead to validate release criteria and testing completion
- Collaborates with Developers on deployment automation and rollback procedures
- Coordinates with Product Manager on feature flags and phased rollouts

---

## QA Lead

### Role Summary
QA Lead owns test strategy and quality standards for deliverables. They ensure all features meet acceptance criteria and Definition of Done before release.

### Responsibilities
- Draft and maintain test plans aligned with project scope
- Coordinate manual and automated testing efforts
- Gather, triage, and track bug reports
- Ensure Definition of Done is met for all deliverables
- Define quality metrics and monitor test coverage
- Advocate for quality improvements and best practices

### Goals
- High confidence in release quality with comprehensive test coverage
- Early detection of defects to reduce rework
- Continuous improvement of testing processes and automation

### Typical Communication
- Test plan reviews with Product Manager and Developers
- Bug triage meetings and quality status reports
- Release readiness sign-offs with Release Manager
- Quality metrics dashboards and retrospective feedback

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Developers on test automation and coverage
- Works with Release Manager to validate release readiness criteria
- Partners with Product Manager to understand acceptance criteria and validate requirements
- Engages Business Analyst to clarify feature specifications and edge cases

---

## Business Analyst

### Role Summary
Business Analyst reviews and refines business requirements, translating stakeholder needs into actionable backlog items. They bridge the gap between business objectives and technical implementation.

### Responsibilities
- Work with Product Manager to gather and process stakeholder feedback
- Document detailed feature requirements and user stories
- Analyze gaps in current processes or product capabilities
- Facilitate requirements workshops and stakeholder interviews
- Create process flows, data models, and business rules documentation
- Validate that delivered features meet business objectives

### Goals
- Clear, complete requirements that minimize rework
- Strong alignment between stakeholder needs and delivered features
- Continuous improvement of requirements processes

### Typical Communication
- Requirements workshops with stakeholders and Product Manager
- User story refinement sessions with Developers
- Acceptance criteria validation with QA Lead
- Business impact analysis and feasibility discussions

### Interactions
- Engages with stakeholders to understand business needs and constraints
- Partners with Product Manager on backlog prioritization and roadmap planning
- Collaborates with QA Lead to ensure requirements are testable
- Works with UX/UI Designer to validate user experience aligns with business goals

---

## Scrum Master

### Role Summary
Scrum Master facilitates agile ceremonies and removes process impediments. They coach the team on agile practices and foster a culture of continuous improvement.

### Responsibilities
- Organize and facilitate sprint planning, standups, and retrospectives
- Coach team members on agile principles and practices
- Identify and remove impediments to team progress
- Shield the team from external distractions during sprints
- Track and visualize team metrics (velocity, burndown, etc.)
- Foster psychological safety and team collaboration

### Goals
- Self-organizing, high-performing teams
- Continuous process improvement and learning
- Predictable delivery with sustainable pace

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint ceremonies
- One-on-ones to identify blockers and coach team members
- Retrospective facilitation and action item tracking
- Escalation of systemic impediments to leadership

### Interactions
- Supports team collaboration and works with Project Manager to escalate blockers
- Partners with Product Manager on backlog refinement and sprint planning
- Collaborates with all team members to identify and remove impediments
- Coordinates with other Scrum Masters for cross-team dependencies

---

## UX/UI Designer

### Role Summary
UX/UI Designer ensures user experience and interface design quality throughout delivery. They advocate for users and create intuitive, accessible designs.

### Responsibilities
- Create wireframes, user flows, and visual design assets
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Validate designs through user feedback and metrics
- Maintain design systems and component libraries
- Collaborate on accessibility and responsive design
- Iterate on designs based on user feedback and analytics

### Goals
- Intuitive, accessible user experiences that delight users
- Consistent design language across products
- Data-informed design decisions validated by user research

### Typical Communication
- Design reviews and critiques with Product Manager and stakeholders
- Collaboration sessions with Developers on implementation feasibility
- Usability test readouts and design iterations
- Design system documentation and component specifications

### Interactions
- Collaborates with Product Manager on feature scoping and user needs
- Works closely with Developers on design implementation and handoff
- Partners with Business Analyst to understand business requirements and user workflows
- Engages with QA Lead to validate design implementation and user experience quality

- Each persona can be used as a persona prompt for Copilot Spaces to shape role-specific guidance.
- These expanded roles reflect modern cross-functional team structures and enhance accountability across the project lifecycle.
